About MAIVI

Our Mission

At MAIVI, we're revolutionizing the architectural design process by bridging the gap between conceptual thinking and technical implementation. Our mission is to empower architects and designers to transform their ideas into reality with unprecedented speed and precision.

We believe that creativity shouldn't be constrained by technical limitations. By harnessing the power of artificial intelligence, we're making advanced architectural modeling accessible to everyone, regardless of their coding expertise.

The Technology

Our platform combines cutting-edge natural language processing with deep expertise in architectural software integration. We've developed proprietary algorithms that understand architectural intent and translate it into executable code.

The core of our system is built on advanced machine learning models trained specifically on architectural terminology, spatial relationships, and construction principles. This specialized training allows our AI to understand complex design requirements and generate appropriate code solutions.

By integrating directly with industry-standard tools like Rhino, Grasshopper, and Revit, we ensure that our technology fits seamlessly into existing architectural workflows while dramatically accelerating the design process.

Benefits for Architects

Time Efficiency

Reduce modeling time from hours to minutes by describing designs in natural language instead of manual modeling.

Design Exploration

Quickly iterate through multiple design options by simply changing your text descriptions.

Technical Accessibility

Create complex parametric designs without needing to learn programming or scripting.

Workflow Integration

Seamlessly move from concept to BIM with automatic conversion between platforms.
